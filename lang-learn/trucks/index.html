    Trucks

    Article

    Trucks do important work. Dumps trucks carry away sand and rocks.
    Cement trucks have a barrel that turns round and round.
    They deliver cement to workers who are making sidewalks.
    Fire trucks carry water hoses and firefighters.
    Gasoline is delivered in large tank trucks.
    Flatbed trucks carry wood to the people who are building houses.

    Question

    Find the sentence in the story that tells the main idea.
    Write it in the circle below.
    Then draw a line from the main idea to all the trucks that were described in the story.
